Careers

Sous Chef

Full-time

Vancouver, BC

Salem's Steakhouse is seeking a highly skilled and motivated Sous Chef to join our culinary team. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in a similar role, showcasing strong leadership and organizational skills. Responsibilities include assisting the Executive and Head Chefs in menu planning, overseeing kitchen operations, managing kitchen staff, and ensuring the highest standards of food quality and presentation.

The Sous Chef will also be responsible for inventory management, kitchen sanitation, and maintaining a positive and collaborative kitchen environment. Culinary creativity, attention to detail, and a passion for delivering exceptional dining experiences are essential for success in this role.

Skills, Education & Competencies:

  • Culinary Expertise
  • Leadership and Communication
  • Time management
  • Adaptability

Pay Rate:
$40/hour

Line Cook

Full-time

Vancouver, BC

Salem's Steakhouse is looking for dedicated and detail-oriented Line Cooks to join our dynamic kitchen team. The successful candidates will be responsible for executing menu items with precision and consistency, adhering to the highest standards of food quality and presentation.

Line Cooks will collaborate closely with the Sous Chef and other kitchen staff, ensuring a smooth and efficient operation. Experience in a high-volume, fast-paced kitchen is preferred, and the ability to work well under pressure is crucial. A passion for culinary excellence, strong teamwork skills, and a commitment to maintaining a clean and organized work environment are key attributes for success in this role.

Skills, Education & Competencies:

  • Culinary Proficiency
  • Teamwork
  • Attention to Detail
  • Adaptability

Pay Rate:
$25/hour

Front House

Full-time

Vancouver, BC

Salem's Steakhouse is currently hiring enthusiastic and customer-focused individuals for Front of House positions. As a vital part of our hospitality team, Front of House staff will be responsible for delivering exceptional customer service, taking orders, and ensuring a positive dining experience for our guests.

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills are essential, as Front of House staff will interact with customers, handle reservations, and collaborate with kitchen and management teams. Attention to detail, a friendly demeanor, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment are crucial for success in this role. Previous experience in the hospitality industry is preferred, but a positive attitude and a willingness to learn are equally valued.

Skills, Education & Competencies:

  • Customer service
  • Communication Skills
  • Problem Solving
  • Positivity and Professionalism

Pay Rate:
$35/hour
