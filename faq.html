FAQs

Q: What are your operating hours?

A: We are open from Monday to Saturday, 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Q: Is there a dress code for dining at Salem Steakhouse?

A: We recommend smart casual attire, but feel free to come as you are!

What are the different cuts of meat Salem’s Steakhouse serve?

Sirloin
A lower fat cut of meat from the sirloin section that results in a tender and flavorful sensation.

Filet Mignon
Cut from the backbone where the steak carries little weight, it results in a soft and tender cut of meat that delicately melts in your mouth.

Ribeye
The most marbled cut with a flavourful yet chewy sensation. Cut from the center of the rub section, served boneless.

Q: Do you offer vegetarian options on the menu?

A: Yes, we have a variety of delicious vegetarian dishes available.

Q: Can I make a reservation for a large group?

A: For those considering a dining experience with us, we can accommodate parties of up to 12, depending on our current occupancy. Feel free to reach out for personalized arrangements or event reservations here.

Q: Does Salem's Steakhouse do delivery?

A: Regrettably, we currently do not offer delivery services at Salem’s Steakhouse. However, we are actively working on introducing an exclusive delivery option soon.

Q: Are gift cards available for purchase?

A: Yes, you can purchase gift cards at our restaurant or online on our website.

Are there any scheduled events or live performances?

Every Friday at 8:00pm, immerse yourself in the ambiance of live jazz performed by Salem’s in-house band.
