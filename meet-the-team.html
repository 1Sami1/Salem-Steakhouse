Meet The Team

Executive Chef

Jeff Lee is the creative force behind Salem's Steakhouse, bringing an impressive 20 years of culinary expertise to the table. With a passion for crafting unforgettable dining experiences, Chef Lee meticulously curates each menu, showcasing his commitment to using the finest locally-sourced ingredients. His innovative approach to steakhouse classics and dedication to culinary excellence has earned him a reputation as a trailblazer in the world of fine dining.

Head Chef

Michael Green is the culinary visionary responsible for translating Chef Green's concepts into reality. With a background rooted in both classical techniques and modern trends, Chef Green brings a wealth of experience to the kitchen. His skillful execution and ability to infuse dishes with unique flavors elevate Salem's Steakhouse to a realm of gastronomic delight. Whether it's a perfectly seared steak or an artfully crafted appetizer, Chef Green's culinary artistry leaves a lasting impression on every plate.

General Manager

At the helm of the restaurant's overall operations is General Manager Alex Garcia. With a wealth of experience in the hospitality industry, Garcia ensures that Salem's Steakhouse not only delights the palate but also provides an impeccable dining experience. His keen attention to detail, commitment to customer satisfaction, and adept management skills make him an integral part of the team. Under the guidance of this dynamic trio, Salem's Steakhouse stands as a culinary destination where exceptional food, innovation, and service converge to create an unparalleled dining haven.
